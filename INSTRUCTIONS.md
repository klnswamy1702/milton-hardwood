# Milton Hardwood Floors — Website Direction

---

## Goal

- Create a premium, modern, and professional flooring showroom website
- Focus on showcasing products, completed projects, services, and generating quote requests
- Website should feel high-end and trustworthy
- Avoid making it feel like a generic contractor site or heavy ecommerce store

---

## Pages Structure

- Keep the website clean and not overcrowded
- Merge sections/pages where needed
- Not every page needs to be visible in the header navigation

### Suggested Pages

- Home
- About Us
- Products
- Portfolio
- Brands / Partner Brands
- FAQ
- Contact Us

---

## Products

### Main Flooring Categories

- Solid Hardwood
- Engineered Hardwood
- Vinyl Flooring
- Laminate Flooring

### Additional Categories

- Cleaning Supplies
- Repair Products
- Glue & Adhesives
- Underlay

### Notes

- Products are mainly for showcasing inventory/catalog items, not full ecommerce
- Products should support filtering/sorting by:
  - Brand
  - Color
  - Wood Type
  - Finish
  - Width

---

## Portfolio

Showcase previous projects with the following categories:

- Solid Hardwood
- Engineered Hardwood
- Vinyl Flooring
- Laminate Flooring
- Stairs
- Repairs

Each project should support:

- Multiple images
- Category tagging
- Project descriptions

---

## About Us

The About Us page should include:

- Information about the company
- Information about the store/showroom
- Information about the team
- Focus on craftsmanship, quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction

---

## Partner Brands

- Clean section/page featuring partner flooring brands carried by the store
- Display brand logos in a premium visual layout

---

## FAQ

Include frequently asked questions related to:

- Flooring types
- Installation
- Repairs
- Maintenance
- Products
- Estimates

---

## Contact Page

Should include:

- Contact form
- Phone number
- Email
- Google Maps / location embed
- Service areas
- Business hours
- Strong call-to-action for quote requests

---

## Design Direction

- Modern luxury flooring showroom aesthetic
- Clean layout with large spacing
- White / light backgrounds
- Small red accent elements matching the logo and red buttons
- Premium flooring visuals
- Smooth and modern UI with good animations
- Mobile responsive
- Fast and clean experience
- Avoid cluttered layouts and overcomplicated design
- Smooth animations on images, video, footer, and products
