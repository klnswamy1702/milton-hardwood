## About Us

### Company Overview

Our passion for wood runs deep, and we hope to inspire the same enthusiasm in you. Our success in the hardwood flooring business is mainly the result of the authentic products we carry and our exceptional customer service. Milton Hardwood Floors sells great quality flooring — flooring that's made to last. We provide floors that look good, feel good, and are luxurious. Our floors are not commodities but rather luxurious and cream of the crop.

Happy customers matter most to us, and we live by this principle at work. It's how we brand ourselves. Our customers are amazed by what we do for them, and offering a variety of product selections helps boost our popularity. Every product that fills our shelves and leaves our doors represents who we are.

### Why Choose Milton Hardwood

- **Expert Quality** — We take pride in all of our flooring options and remain committed to offering exactly what our clients are in search of. We only provide high-quality flooring solutions — ones we would even want in our own homes. We carry a wide range of materials including vintage hardwood, superior hardwood, and Fuzion, with an equally wide range of colours and finishes.

- **Competitive Pricing** — Incredible flooring doesn't have to break the bank. We strive to provide high-quality and affordable flooring to every client. We will never compromise on quality to find lower prices. Instead, we work with your flooring budget and come up with creative solutions.

- **Customer Care** — Above all, Milton Hardwood is dedicated to offering the best customer care. We take an interest in your flooring projects because we want to make sure whichever option you choose lasts. We offer flexible meeting options including in-person, over-the-phone, virtual, and email correspondence.

## Shipping Policy

### Delivery Time

- All orders are processed within the following business day
- Delivery dates are estimated and cannot be guaranteed due to variances in the trucking industry
- Order well in advance of your installation date
- If hiring a third-party installer, book installation only after receiving the product
- Shipments are tracked and customers receive updated delivery information

### How Orders Are Shipped

- Orders under 150 lbs. ship via UPS Ground (upgrade options available at checkout)
- Orders 150 lbs. or greater ship via freight carrier
- For large orders over 3,000 lbs. (approx. 1,200 sq ft), having a forklift on-site reduces shipping costs
- Drivers are not responsible for unloading — arrange help in advance
- If your area is difficult for trucks to access, call 1-833-408-4301 to arrange alternatives

### Freight Delivery Requirements

- A responsible adult must be present at the time of delivery — signature required
- Freight is unloaded curbside only — delivery personnel cannot enter the home or assist beyond curbside
- If no adult is present or the carrier cannot reach you to coordinate, additional shipping and storage fees apply
- Re-routed shipments are subject to additional charges — call for rate information

### Inspection at Delivery

- Inspect all materials and sign for your order upon delivery
- Note any damages or shortages on the bill of lading before signing
- Look for rounded corners, denting, and gouges
- Contact Milton Hardwood within 3 business days of delivery if discrepancies are found
- If damages are discovered after signing for no damages, contact the delivery company within 48 hours
- Milton Hardwood cannot file claims more than 15 business days after delivery
- Do not install material until fully satisfied — installation is considered final acceptance

## Refund & Return Policy

### General Terms

- Milton Hardwood stands behind all products sold
- Customers must inspect material upon arrival and note any issues on the bill of lading
- Returns are subject to a 25% restocking fee
- Customer is responsible for arranging and paying for return shipment
- Product overages are not automatically eligible for return — a return request may or may not be granted

### How to Return

1. Call 1-833-408-4301 or contact us to initiate a return
2. A Return Authorization Number (RA#) will be issued
3. Customers must obtain an RA# within 14 days of receiving the order
4. Clearly reference the RA# on the outside of the return package
5. Milton Hardwood will coordinate pickup for the return
6. Returns without a pre-authorized RA# will be refused

### Return Conditions

- Merchandise must be returned within 10 days of purchase
- Items must be unused, like-new condition
- Full/unopened boxes only — opened merchandise is not eligible
- A copy of the customer receipt and original packing slip must accompany all returns
- Refund is issued to the original credit card only
- Minimum return is 4 cartons for online purchases — returns of fewer than 4 cartons cannot be authorized

### Non-Returnable Items

- Opened, installed, soiled, damaged, or used items
- Accessories (transition moulding, underlayment, floor care products, installation kits, etc.)
- Final Sale or Clearance items
- Special offerings or discounted items
- Special order, custom-made, or cut roll merchandise
- Items delivered more than 14 days ago

### Cancelled Orders

- If the order has not shipped: call 1-833-408-4301 — cancellation at no charge
- If the order has already shipped: a 25% restocking fee plus applicable freight charges apply
- Refused deliveries: customer is responsible for 25% restocking fee and all return freight charges
